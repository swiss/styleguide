Barrier-free Accessibility

All of the Confederations Internet resources must be designed in such a way as to enable people with disabilities to use them without restrictions. The Federal Administration is committed to fulfilling the requirements of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0 to conformance level AA.
